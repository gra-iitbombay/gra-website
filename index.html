About Us

The Group for Rural Activities (GRA) is a voluntary student organization at IIT Bombay dedicated to exposing the IITB community to the diverse facets of rural India...

Camps & Immersion Programs

GRA organizes 4-5 day camps to various regions, facilitating interactions with villagers and NGOs to understand their work and challenges...

Bicycle Donation Drive

An initiative to collect unused bicycles from graduating students and distribute them to needy children in rural and tribal areas...

Talks by Social Workers

GRA hosts talks by esteemed social workers to inspire and educate students about rural development...

Teach Me Program

A platform designed to support underprivileged students from Phule Nagar near IIT Bombay...
